Who We Are

A Legacy of Trust and Impeccable Standards.

Incorporated as a full-fledged company in 1986, HC Cleaning Services Sdn Bhd (399404-K) has grown from a local enterprise into one of the most established facility management corporations in Malaysia. With a paid-up capital of RM250,000.00 and an operational workforce of over 300 dedicated personnel, we do not compromise on quality.

We provide comprehensive janitorial, environmental maintenance, and specialized high-rise solutions. Together with our subsidiaries, HTC Eco resources Sdn bhd and Friendly Eco Services, we offer complete property care from the top floor down to the gardens.
